Reddit + Videos:

PseudoAgriculture - I made it because vanilla Project Zomboid doesn't allow you to grow most crops in the autumn, and in real life Louisville you can. So I did the research and adjusted growing seasons.
PseudoButterCandle - I made it because there wasn't a renewable portable light source in vanilla Project Zomboid. Butter is a renewable fuel, and people make emergency candles from it.
PseudoClay - I made it because there isn't enough clay in vanilla Project Zomboid, and clay is a lot easier to find in real life
PseudoGritsPorridgePottage - I made it because corn/wheat/peas should have more value in recipes, especially in recipies that use other renewable food sources
PseudoSaltRecipes - I made it because there is no realistic food preservation in vanilla project zomboid without refrigeration
PseudoSaltWell42_19 - I made it because central Kentucky was the largest supplier of salt west of the Appalachian mountains until the civil war. Salt should be easy to extract.
PseudoTortillas - I made it because tortillas are easy to make in real life, and there aren't enough flour recipes that only require renewable ingredients

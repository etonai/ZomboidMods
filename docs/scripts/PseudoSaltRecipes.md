I don't know why Project Zomboid makes you use vinegar to preserve food, and only for 60+30 days.
In real life, all it really takes is a bunch of salt.
That's why I made PseudonymousEd's Salt-Preserved Foods mod, to lean into using salt to preserve food like humans have been doing for thousands of years.
Either salt meats directly, or stick vegetables into glass or clay with salt and water and let lactofermentation handle the preservation.
Now I have jars of sauerkraut and other lactofermented vegetables, to help get me through the winter.

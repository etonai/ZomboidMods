In Project Zomboid, you can't grow crops like Cabbages, Peas, or Carrots in the late summer or autumn even though in real life Kentucky these are great late summer or autumn crops.
That's why I made the Realistic Kentucky Farming Calendar mod, using data from Almanac.com and the University of Kentucky website to adjust the crop growing seasons.

Did you know that Kentucky's first industry was Saltmaking?
It was centered in Bullitt's Lick, just a dozen miles east of Muldraugh - that's closer than the Louisville or even the Valley Mall!
It supplied Illinois, Tennessee, even New Orleans and Pittsburgh 

That's why I made PseudonymousEd's Salt Wel; mod - process salt the way they did in the old days.

Dig a hole in certain locations to create a salt well. Collect brine from those salt wells and boil them off, leaving salt.

Now my survivors have enough salt to preserve all the meat and crops they grow.
